Concept Overview

A platform that enables small delivery dispatch companies to manage their riders, accept delivery requests, monitor real-time locations, and provide a seamless customer experience through an intuitive interface.

Who It's For

  • Dispatch Companies: Manage riders, earnings, and operations from a central dashboard.
  • Riders: Accept jobs, navigate routes, and track earnings via mobile apps.
  • Customers: Book deliveries, track packages, and leave feedback easily.
