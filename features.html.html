Key Features

For Dispatch Companies

  • Company Dashboard to manage riders and track activity.
  • Live GPS Tracking of rider locations.
  • Automated Dispatching based on proximity and availability.
  • Earnings Reports and Rider Performance Analytics.
  • Customer Order History and Feedback System.

For Riders

  • Mobile App to accept and complete delivery tasks.
  • GPS Navigation and Route Optimization.
  • Earnings Summary by Day or Week.

For Customers

  • Website/App to book deliveries from trusted dispatchers.
  • Compare prices and service providers.
  • Live Package Tracking with real-time updates.
  • Leave Ratings and Reviews after each delivery.
